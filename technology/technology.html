The Journey

How SeleneX Works

A Trustworthy AI Super-Agent for Early Cancer Detection & Personalized Care—operating as a coordinated system that reasons across medical data, patient context, and clinical guidelines.

Stage 1 — Foundation

Intelligent Patient Understanding

Building a complete, interpretable patient picture

SeleneX begins by creating a holistic digital understanding of the patient, not just a snapshot. It securely ingests and organizes data into a Patient Knowledge Graph—a structured, interpretable map.

  • Medical history and electronic health records
  • Symptoms and patient-reported experiences
  • Demographics, hormonal history, and risk factors

Why This Matters

Early ovarian cancer rarely presents as a single red flag. SeleneX catches patterns across weak, fragmented signals that are often missed in routine care.

Stage 2 — Diagnosis

Progressive Diagnostic Reasoning

From low-invasive signals to deeper clinical certainty

Rather than ordering every test upfront, SeleneX follows a progressive diagnostic pathway similar to expert clinicians, updating risk scores and explaining each step.

  • Initial risk estimation using history and symptoms
  • Targeted lab and biomarker analysis when risk rises
  • Advanced imaging only when justified
  • Genomic profiling for high-confidence cases

Why This Matters

SeleneX reasons dynamically, adapting its diagnostic depth to each individual—avoiding unnecessary procedures while shortening time-to-diagnosis.

Stage 3 — Synthesis

Explainable AI Decision Synthesis

Turning complex data into clinician-ready insight

SeleneX synthesizes all inputs into a clear diagnostic assessment with human-readable explanations, visual highlights, and confidence measures.

  • Likelihood of cancer with confidence scores
  • Probable subtype and aggressiveness
  • Key drivers behind each conclusion
  • Visual explanation of what influenced decisions

Clinician-in-the-Loop

SeleneX supports decisions, never replaces professional judgment. Every conclusion is traceable and auditable.

Stage 4 — Treatment

Personalized Treatment via Digital Twin

From diagnosis to individualized therapy planning

SeleneX creates a digital twin of the patient—a virtual model reflecting tumor biology, genetic profile, and clinical context for simulation.

  • Test multiple treatment strategies virtually
  • Forecast outcomes, side effects, and recurrence risk
  • Compare options based on survival & quality of life
  • Explore "what-if" scenarios before real decisions

Why This Matters

Treatment responses vary dramatically. SeleneX moves oncology from protocol-driven care to truly individualized therapy selection.

Stage 5 — Continuity

Adaptive Follow-Up & Long-Term Care

Care doesn't stop at treatment

SeleneX continues to support patients after treatment begins, adapting follow-up intensity based on each patient's evolving risk profile.

  • Monitor biomarkers, imaging, and follow-up data
  • Detect early signs of recurrence
  • Update risk models as new data arrives
  • Enable earlier intervention when problems emerge

Continuous Adaptation

The system learns and adapts with each patient interaction, getting smarter over time.

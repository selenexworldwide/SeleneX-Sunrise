# SeleneX Projects & Case Studies

## Overview

SeleneX specializes in multimodal AI solutions for early cancer detection and precision care. Our projects focus on integrating imaging, biomarkers, molecular signals, and clinical data to improve patient outcomes through earlier diagnosis and personalized treatment pathways.

### Synthetic Data Generation for Rare Subtypes

**Healthcare - Data Augmentation**

**Addressing Data Scarcity in High-Grade Serous Ovarian Carcinoma**

Using diffusion models and GANs to generate compliant synthetic data that addresses limitations in rare cancer subtype datasets while maintaining privacy and bias-free training.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Expanded training datasets for rare subtypes
- Maintained EHDS metadata compliance
- Improved model robustness for underrepresented populations
- Enabled scalable training in low-resource settings

## Our Process

### Streamlined and Validated Approach

A proven methodology designed for healthcare AI projects that prioritize safety, compliance, and clinical impact.

#### 1. Discovery & Clinical Assessment

We begin by understanding your clinical workflows, data infrastructure, and regulatory requirements. This phase includes stakeholder interviews, system audits, and compliance gap analysis.

#### 2. Research & Proposal

Our team conducts thorough research into your specific use case, reviewing relevant literature, analyzing data availability, and crafting a custom strategy that aligns with clinical goals and regulatory standards.

#### 3. Development & Integration

Our development follows agile methodologies adapted for healthcare environments. We prioritize safety, explainability, and seamless integration with existing hospital systems (PACS, EHR).

#### 4. Validation & Deployment

We ensure rigorous validation, including bias testing, explainability verification, and clinical validation. Deployment is designed for minimal disruption to clinical workflows.

#### 5. Monitoring & Support

Ongoing support includes model monitoring, performance tracking, compliance updates, and continuous improvement based on real-world clinical feedback.

# SeleneX Glossary

## Medical & Clinical Terms

### Cancer Staging
- **Stage I–II:** Early-stage cancer, localized to the organ of origin
- **Stage III–IV:** Advanced-stage cancer, may have spread to nearby tissues or distant organs
- **HGSOC:** High-Grade Serous Ovarian Carcinoma

### Diagnostic Modalities
- **Imaging:** CT (Computed Tomography), MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging), Ultrasound
- **Biomarkers:** Measurable indicators of biological processes or conditions
- **Liquid Biopsy:** Non-invasive test using blood or other body fluids to detect cancer
- **cfDNA:** Cell-free DNA found in blood plasma
- **VOCs:** Volatile Organic Compounds (detected in breath analysis)

### Clinical Terms
- **EHR:** Electronic Health Records
- **Oncology:** Branch of medicine dealing with cancer
- **Pathology:** Study of disease through examination of tissues
- **Radiology:** Medical imaging for diagnosis and treatment

---

## AI & Technology Terms

### AI Concepts
- **Multimodal AI:** AI systems that process multiple types of data (images, text, molecular data)
- **Explainable AI (XAI):** AI systems that provide understandable explanations for their decisions
- **Federated Learning:** Machine learning approach where models are trained across decentralized data without sharing raw data
- **Digital Twins:** Virtual representations of patients for simulation and prediction

### Technical Terms
- **GNN:** Graph Neural Networks
- **PKG:** Proprietary Knowledge Graph
- **LLM:** Large Language Model
- **GAN:** Generative Adversarial Network
- **RL:** Reinforcement Learning

### Data Terms
- **Omics:** Comprehensive study of biological molecules (genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics)
- **Pseudonymisation:** Data processing technique that replaces identifying information with pseudonyms
- **Data Sovereignty:** Concept that data is subject to the laws of the country where it is located

---

## Compliance & Regulatory Terms

### Regulations
- **GDPR:** General Data Protection Regulation (EU data protection law)
- **EU AI Act:** European Union Artificial Intelligence Act
- **DPIA:** Data Protection Impact Assessment
- **MDR:** Medical Device Regulation (EU)
- **HIPAA:** Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (US)
- **EHDS:** European Health Data Space

### Compliance Terms
- **Privacy-by-Design:** Approach that embeds privacy into system design from the start
- **Security-by-Default:** Security measures enabled by default
- **High-Risk Category:** AI systems classified as high-risk under EU AI Act
- **External Audit:** Independent review of systems and processes

---

## Platform-Specific Terms

### SeleneX Features
- **DeepCheck™:** SeleneX's diagnostic engine
- **Precision Treatment Navigator:** AI-assisted treatment personalization tool
- **Multimodal Fusion:** Integration of different data types into unified analysis
- **Progressive Data Escalation:** Starting with non-invasive tests before advancing to more intensive procedures

### Data Sources
- **Imaging Data:** CT, MRI, Ultrasound scans
- **Molecular Signals:** Genomic, transcriptomic, proteomic data
- **Clinical Context:** Patient history, symptoms, EHR data
- **Patient-Reported Factors:** Symptoms and observations reported by patients

# SeleneX Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Brand Name
**SeleneX Labs**

### Tagline
**Deep Intelligence for Early Disease Discovery & Precision Care**

### Mission Statement
SeleneX is a multimodal, AI-powered deep-intelligence platform that analyzes imaging, biomarkers, molecular signals, and clinical context to detect cancer earlier — at stage I–II — and personalize treatment pathways.

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

- **White (#FFFFFF)** — Clinical, clean, medical
- **Deep Indigo (#1D1B4F)** — Medical-scientific, trustworthy
- **Soft Blue Gradient (#6377FF → #8AA8FF)** — AI, technology, innovation
- **Thin Silver/Grey (#DBDBDB)** — Minimalist molecular lines

### Typography

- **Primary Fonts:** Satoshi, Inter, or Circular
- **Secondary Fonts:** IBM Plex Sans or Helvetica Neue

### Visual Motifs

Use **extremely minimalist**:

- DNA double-helix outlines (1–2px thickness)
- RNA single-strand curves
- Molecule graphs (very sparse, node-based)
- Circles and curves representing data layers
- Broken strands merging → symbolizing multimodal fusion

### What NOT to Use

- Detailed biological drawings
- Real biopsy images
- Omics heatmap screenshots
- Any sequence-like patterns that resemble real genomic signatures

## Tone of Voice

### Key Principles

1. **Professional yet Accessible** — Medical accuracy without jargon overload
2. **Confident but Humble** — Strong claims backed by evidence, not hype
3. **Patient-Centered** — Always emphasize patient outcomes and safety
4. **Transparent** — Clear about capabilities and limitations
5. **Ethical First** — Privacy, security, and compliance are non-negotiable

### Messaging Pillars

1. **Early Detection Saves Lives** — Focus on Stage I–II detection
2. **Multimodal Intelligence** — Integration of multiple data sources
3. **Privacy by Design** — No raw data leaves hospitals
4. **Explainable AI** — Clinicians understand the reasoning
5. **Global Collaboration** — International team and partnerships

## Key Messages

### What SeleneX Does

- Early cancer detection (Stage I–II)
- Personalized treatment insights
- Multimodal data fusion (imaging + biomarkers + molecular + clinical)
- High-level explanation ONLY (no IP details)

### Core Value Propositions

- **Earlier Detection** — Detect cancer at Stage I–II, not Stage III–IV
- **Better Outcomes** — Personalized treatment pathways
- **Privacy First** — No raw data leaves the hospital
- **Explainable** — Clinicians understand AI decisions
- **Compliant** — GDPR + EU AI Act aligned

## Content Guidelines

### Do's

- Emphasize patient outcomes and safety
- Highlight privacy and compliance
- Use clear, accessible language
- Focus on benefits, not just features
- Include ethical considerations in all messaging

### Don'ts

- Reveal proprietary algorithms or IP
- Make unsubstantiated medical claims
- Use overly technical jargon without explanation
- Show real patient data or images
- Overpromise on capabilities

## Target Audiences

### Primary Audiences

1. **Hospitals & Clinics** — Decision makers, IT, clinical staff
2. **Research Partners** — Academic institutions, research organizations
3. **Investors** — VCs, strategic investors
4. **Regulatory Bodies** — Compliance reviewers, auditors

### Messaging by Audience

**For Hospitals:**
- Focus on integration, privacy, clinical outcomes
- Emphasize no disruption to workflows
- Highlight compliance and security

**For Research Partners:**
- Emphasize collaboration opportunities
- Highlight data sovereignty
- Focus on scientific validation

**For Investors:**
- Market opportunity and impact
- Team credentials and expertise
- Regulatory compliance and IP protection

## Compliance Messaging

Always include when relevant:

- "GDPR compliant"
- "EU AI Act aligned"
- "Privacy-by-design"
- "No raw data leaves the hospital"
- "External audits and compliance oversight"

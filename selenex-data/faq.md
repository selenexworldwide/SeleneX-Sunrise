# SeleneX Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is SeleneX?
SeleneX is a multimodal, AI-powered deep-intelligence platform designed for early cancer detection and precision care. It analyzes imaging, biomarkers, molecular signals, and clinical context to detect cancer at stage I–II and personalize treatment pathways.

### What makes SeleneX different?
SeleneX uses multimodal data fusion, combining radiology, blood biomarkers, genomic signals, clinical history, and patient-reported factors into a single interpretable analysis. Unlike tools that focus on isolated modalities, SeleneX provides a holistic view while maintaining strict privacy and compliance standards.

### What types of cancer does SeleneX detect?
SeleneX is initially focused on ovarian cancer detection, with particular emphasis on high-grade serous ovarian carcinoma (HGSOC).

## Privacy & Security

### How does SeleneX protect patient data?
- No raw data leaves the hospital
- All data is fully pseudonymised and encrypted
- Privacy-by-design and security-by-default architecture
- Federated or hybrid learning options available

### Is SeleneX GDPR compliant?
Yes, SeleneX is GDPR compliant and aligned with EU AI Act requirements. We undergo external independent audits to ensure ongoing compliance.

### Where is patient data stored?
Patient data remains within the hospital's infrastructure. SeleneX processes data without requiring it to leave the medical center, ensuring data sovereignty.

## Technology & Integration

### How does SeleneX integrate with existing hospital systems?
SeleneX integrates with existing PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication Systems) and EHR (Electronic Health Records) systems without disrupting current workflows.

### What data sources does SeleneX use?
SeleneX integrates:
- Imaging data (CT, MRI, Ultrasound)
- Blood biomarkers and lab results
- Genomic/molecular signals
- Electronic health records
- Patient-reported symptoms

### Does SeleneX require new clinical procedures?
No, SeleneX works with existing clinical data and procedures. It does not require hospitals to implement new patient interventions.

## Clinical & Medical

### What stage of cancer can SeleneX detect?
SeleneX is designed to detect cancer at stage I–II, significantly earlier than traditional methods which often catch cancer at stage III–IV.

### How accurate is SeleneX?
SeleneX has been validated on datasets exceeding 10,000 records. The platform aims to improve early detection rates by over 20% compared to traditional screening methods.

### Can clinicians understand how SeleneX makes decisions?
Yes, explainability is a core feature. SeleneX provides:
- Natural language explanations
- Attention heatmaps
- Confidence scoring
- Causal reasoning
- Step-by-step decision rationales

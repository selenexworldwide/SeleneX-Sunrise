# 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM (with DNA/RNA elements)

## Color Palette

- White (#FFFFFF) — clinical
- Deep Indigo (#1D1B4F) — medical-scientific
- Soft Blue Gradient (#6377FF → #8AA8FF) — AI
- Thin Silver/Grey (#DBDBDB) — minimalist molecular lines

## Motifs

Use **extremely minimalist**:

- DNA double-helix outlines (1–2px thickness)
- RNA single-strand curves
- molecule graphs (very sparse, node-based)
- circles and curves representing data layers
- broken strands merging → symbolizing multimodal fusion

## Do NOT use:

- detailed biological drawings
- real biopsy images
- omics heatmap screenshots
- any sequence-like patterns that resemble real genomic signatures

## Typography

- Primary: **Satoshi**, **Inter**, or **Circular**
- Secondary: **IBM Plex Sans** or **Helvetica Neue**

## Animation Ideas

- Slowly rotating helix outline
- Soft "data pulses" traveling through an RNA-like curve
- Circles merging to represent multimodal fusion
- Clinical interface mockups (no real patient data)

## How It Works Section

### Step 1: Secure Data Intake
Integrates imaging, labs, and available clinical data within each medical center.

### Step 2: Molecular & Clinical Encoding
Transforms signals into structured representations without exposing raw data.

### Step 3: Multimodal Fusion
A proprietary AI engine merges cross-modal signals into unified reasoning.

### Step 4: Clinical Insights
Generates interpretable early-detection outputs and personalized treatment pathways.

### Step 5: Continuous Medical Validation
Integrated fairness, safety, and ethical monitoring.
